So what is the main problem and why we need to pay extra attention to our customer screening

Sanctions screening is a top challenge for many organizations. Firms that continue to do business with sanctioned governments, legal entities, or individuals face substantial fines. But financial consequences are not the only concern. Global investors and citizens demand genuine corporate social responsibility and are quick to distance themselves from firms that don't measure up.

The customer identity management area, which includes sanction screening of the clients, is one of the most expanded sectors throughout the years. As we see from the graph, there is a new sanction that applies to businesses every day, and companies need to prepare internal procedures and mechanisms to prevent getting into business relationships with clients that can be listed into sanction lists.

Our business plan is focused on targeting small to medium-sized financial institutions that are struggling to keep up with the ever-changing compliance landscape. We plan to achieve this by leveraging our expertise and technology to offer cost-effective solutions that are tailored to each institution's specific needs.
